About Kerala Government

Kerala is governed as a state within the Republic of India, with a parliamentary system led by the Chief Minister and overseen by the Governor. The state government manages public services such as health, education, agriculture, transport, and social welfare, while working through state ministries to deliver policies for citizens. The Kerala Government 2026 is led by Chief Minister V D Satheesan under the United Democratic Front (UDF). The new government focuses on development, public welfare, education, healthcare, employment, and modern infrastructure across the state. The cabinet includes experienced leaders handling important departments such as Home Affairs, Finance, Agriculture, Health, and Forest. The government aims to improve the quality of life of people in Kerala through transparent administration and people-friendly policies.
